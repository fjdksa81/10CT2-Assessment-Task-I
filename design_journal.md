# **Part I  - Project Proposal**

### **Design Brief**
For this design project, I will be developing a website based on the book "To Be The Foundation - The Story of Gosford High School", mainly focused at students and alumni, teaching about small stories, and interesting factoids from school history.
### **Book Choice and Justification**
To Be The Foundation - The Story of Gosford High School, is, obviously, a book close to my heart, focused on Gosford High School. Written by Pamela Williams and John Lewis for the turn of the Millenium, writes on the entire history of the school. A difficult task, the book interests me greatly, and presents a fun challenge to design around.
### **User Experience Task**
I plan to create a User Experience website or desktop app on Figma, furthering the educational goal of the book by writing about included and excluded stories from the history of GHS. A website is both accessible, and known to many students, a simple design available to the public and students alike.
### **Target Market**
The main target audience for the user design is GHS Students, and Former Students, appealing to a sense of either curiousity or nostalgia, the subject matter (Gosford High School), obviously familiar to them. The design being a website or desktop app is best for accessability, allowing anyone with a laptop, desktop, or even a screen to hook up a phone, to easily explore school history, which all students must have access to for their learning anyway.
### **Software and Tools**
I will use Figma mainly for the prototyping and development of the App, suitable both for it's easy tools, prior knowledge to me, and ease of testing, allowing new builds to easily be pushed to testers. For specific graphics, I may use either Canva or paint.net, using school subscriptions to make use of familiar programs to work around the limitations of Figma.
